Mick Taylor

Black Phillip

This loveable scamp was the first entrant into the Hall of Fame, and for good reason. His movie ‘Sinister’ sat atop the rankings for much of Horror Night’s existence, and currently sits 2nd overall. ‘Sinister’ still holds the record amongst its competitors for the Scariest rated film.

If Mick Taylor wasn’t already a guarantee for the Hall of Fame with his signature laugh and classic one-liners such as “Head on a Stick”, he cemented his place during ‘Wolf Creek 2’ as he plowed through a group of kangaroo to the tune of ‘In the Jungle’.

“Black Phillip, Black Phillip crown upon his head.” Black Phillip the Goat stole the show (and our hearts) towards the end of ’The Witch’, when it was revealed that he was actually the devil himself and the true antagonist of the movie.


The Hair

Tommy Ross

Rod Williams

Blink and you’ll miss it. The Hair makes only a split second appearance in ‘Don’t Breathe’, floating in a semen filled turkey baster. This tiny detail was an instant fan favourite amongst an otherwise disturbing scene.

It was hard not to have a soft spot for Tommy when he made his appearance in ‘Carrie’, with the teen heartthrob in stark contrast to the rest of his classmates. Tears were shed when he was knocked unconscious by a falling bucket, triggering Carrie’s psychic rage and presumably perishing in the ensuing fire. Rest in Peace Sweet Prince.

“I’m T-S-motherfuckin’-A. We handle shit. Thats what we do. Consider this situation fuckin’ handled”. The latest entry to the Hall of Fame, Rod Williams provided the comic relief in the supernatural thriller ‘Get Out’. The hilarious TSA agent was easily the highlight of the film, and it was only fitting that he was there at the end to save the day.
